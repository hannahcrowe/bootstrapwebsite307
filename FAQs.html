Frequently Asked Questions

When will I receive my ticket after purchasing?

Festival, Camping, and Parking Passes will all be shipped between April 2024 and up until two weeks before the festival. Passes are shipped in particular order and are shipped together if purchased together to one address.

When is the last day to purchase a ticket to have it shipped on time before the festival?

June 5, 2024 is the last day to purchase a ticket and to have it shipped on time before the festival starts.

When will the set times be released?

The set times will be announced the week before the festival starts on Thursday, June 13, 2024 via text if signed up for the announcements and on social media.

Is Firefly a cashless festival?

YES! Firefly is a cashless festival that accepts credit card, debit card, and now contactless payments as well.

What should I bring with me to the festival?

Great question! Please bring your 2024 Firefly Music Festival Wristband, 2024 Firefly Music Festical Camping or Overnight Parking Pass (if applicable), a form of payment such as a debit or credit card, and a government-issued photo i.d. such as a drivers license. We also recommend bringing a comfortable pair of shoes, portable phone charger, sun protection, and sunglasses.

Will there be food vendors to accomodate my dietary restrictions such as vegetarian, gluten-free, etc?

Yes! We provide a variety of food vendors where you will be able to find accomodations for your dietary restrictions. There are many vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, peanut-free vendors. All vendors will have their menus posted in front of their service area for you to view to help your decision when deciding where to eat.

How many vehicles are allowed per campsite?

We offer many types of campsites with lots of different vehicle capacity options. Our Tent Camping, RV Camping, and Glamping options all are restricted to one vehicle per campsite. Our Group Camping option, however, allows for up to four vehicles per Group Camping campsite.
